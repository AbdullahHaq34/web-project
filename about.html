Our History

Founded in 1995, Velocity Motors started as a small workshop in Detroit. With a passion for speed, design, and technology, we grew into one of the most respected performance car manufacturers in North America. Our cars are built with precision, passion, and the pursuit of excellence.

Today, we produce electric and hybrid performance vehicles that blend cutting-edge engineering with sustainable innovation.
